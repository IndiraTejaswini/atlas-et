# Work Order WO-2301 — P-101A Mechanical Seal Replacement

**Reported problem:** Crude leak from seal gland of P-101A observed during routine round at 06:40. Pump stopped, standby P-101B started per SOP-012.

**Work performed:**
- Decontaminated and removed seal cartridge (P/N SLZ-88412).
- Seal faces showed heavy heat checking and carbon face scoring — consistent with dry running or loss of flush cooling.
- New cartridge seal installed, seal chamber flushed.
- Plan 32 flush flow verified after reinstatement: flow indicator sluggish, cleaned strainer, flow restored.

**Findings:** Seal faces overheated. Flush line strainer found 60% blocked with waxy deposits. Strainer cleaning interval is quarterly; OEM manual recommends monthly cleaning when running waxy crude.

**Parts used:** Seal cartridge SLZ-88412 x1.
**Technician:** M. Khan. **Approved by:** A. Verma (Rotating Equipment Engineer).
