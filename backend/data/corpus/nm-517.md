# Near-Miss Report NM-517 — Hot Work Gas Test Lapse

**Event:** During scaffolding pipe modification 20 m from TK-305, welding continued 3.5 hours after the last gas test. SOP-021 and OISD-STD-105 require a repeat gas test every 2 hours. A naphtha sample point was opened 25 m away during the same window. No release reached the work site; detected during a routine safety walk by P. Nair.

**Immediate cause:** Permit renewal gas test skipped; fire watch was unaware of the 2-hour re-test rule.

**Root cause:**
1. Hot work permit training refreshers lapsed for 2 of 6 contract fire watches.
2. SOP-021 is past its 3-yearly review date (Rev 2, 2021; review was due 2024-09) — the re-test interval table on the permit form no longer matches the SOP text.

**Actions:**
1. All hot work stopped pending toolbox retraining — DONE (2025-10-06).
2. Revise SOP-021 and reprint permit forms with consistent 2-hour re-test rule — OPEN, owner P. Nair.
3. Add contractor fire watch competency check to permit issuance — OPEN.

**Learning:** An out-of-date procedure is not a paperwork problem; the stale permit form directly caused a field practice gap.

**Reported by:** P. Nair (Safety Officer).
