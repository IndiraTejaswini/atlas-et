# SOP-021 — Hot Work Permit Procedure

Governing standard: OISD-STD-105 (Work Permit System). Applies to welding, grinding, gas cutting and any spark-producing activity inside process areas.

## Requirements
1. Hot work permit to be issued by Area In-charge and countersigned by Safety Officer.
2. Gas test for hydrocarbons: LEL must read 0% at the work location and within 15 m radius. Repeat gas test every 2 hours or after any process upset.
3. Fire watch with charged hose or 10 kg DCP extinguisher to be present for the full duration and 30 minutes after completion.
4. Sewers, drains and trenches within 15 m to be sealed.
5. Permit validity: one shift maximum. Fresh gas test required for renewal.

## Prohibited Conditions
- No hot work during product transfer operations in the same unit.
- No hot work within 30 m of a hydrocarbon leak or open sample point.

## Records
Completed permits are retained for 12 months and audited quarterly by the safety department per OISD-STD-105 audit requirements.

Note: This procedure is due for its 3-yearly review. Last revision 2021 (Rev 2).
