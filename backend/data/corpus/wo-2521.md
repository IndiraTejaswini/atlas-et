# Work Order WO-2521 — P-101A High Vibration / Bearing Replacement

**Reported problem:** Vibration on P-101A outboard bearing trended up from 4.2 to 8.6 mm/s RMS over two weeks, exceeding the 7.1 mm/s alarm limit from the OEM manual (ISO 10816).

**Work performed:**
- Pump stopped, changeover to P-101B per SOP-012.
- Bearing housing opened: thrust bearing (SKF 7310 BECBM) showed spalling on outer race. Radial bearing serviceable but replaced as a set (bearing kit SKF-7310-KIT).
- Oil found dark with wear debris; last oil change exceeded the 4000 hour OEM interval by roughly 1800 hours.
- Alignment checked and corrected: 0.08 mm offset found at coupling.

**Findings:** Bearing distress attributed to overdue lubrication combined with extended low-flow operation during January turndown (unit ran near 120 m3/hr, below the 130 m3/hr minimum flow stated in the OEM manual Section 8).

**Recommendation:** Add P-101A/B oil change to the 6-monthly PM schedule; enforce minimum-flow line open during turndown.
