# Work Order WO-2734 — E-104 Tube Bundle Cleaning

**Reported problem:** Crude preheat exchanger E-104 outlet temperature dropped 14 °C below design over 4 months, indicating severe fouling; furnace duty increased correspondingly.

**Work performed:**
- E-104 isolated, blinded, and opened under confined space procedure SOP-034.
- Tube bundle pulled and hydrojetted; heavy asphaltene and wax fouling on tube side, consistent with the waxy crude slate introduced in 2024.
- Shell side internals visually inspected. External shell condition referred to inspection — see corrosion findings in INSP-088.
- 6 tubes plugged (out of 480) due to wall thinning at the baffle cuts.

**Findings:** Fouling rate has roughly doubled since the crude slate change. Cleaning interval should move from 3-yearly to 18 months. Tube wall thinning localized at baffles suggests flow-induced erosion.

**Recommendation:** Retube assessment at next turnaround; consider shell-side thickness survey expansion per INSP-088 findings.

**Technician crew:** contract. **Approved by:** S. Iyer (Inspection Engineer).
