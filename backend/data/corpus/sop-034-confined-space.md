# SOP-034 — Confined Space Entry

Governing requirements: Factories Act Section 36 (precautions against dangerous fumes, gases), OISD-STD-105.

## Scope
Applies to entry into vessels, columns, tanks and exchangers including V-302, C-201, TK-305 and E-104 channel/shell during turnaround.

## Requirements
1. Confined space entry permit issued jointly by Area In-charge and Safety Officer.
2. Positive isolation by blinding; valve isolation alone is NOT acceptable.
3. Gas test before entry and every 2 hours: O2 between 19.5% and 23.5%, LEL 0%, H2S below 10 ppm, CO below 25 ppm.
4. Attendant (hole watch) stationed at the manway for the entire entry duration with communication to entrants.
5. Continuous ventilation with eductor or blower; breathing apparatus required if ventilation cannot maintain safe atmosphere.
6. Rescue tripod and retrieval harness at the manway for vertical entries.

## Records
Entry permits retained 12 months. Annual mock rescue drill required; last drill conducted 2025-08-12.
