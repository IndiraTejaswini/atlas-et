# Inspection Report INSP-088 — E-104 CUI Survey

**Scope:** External visual and ultrasonic thickness survey of E-104 shell following insulation damage observed near the south saddle. Conducted per OISD-STD-128 guidance for unfired pressure vessels in CUI-susceptible service (operating 60-175 °C band).

**Findings:**
- Insulation weather jacket damaged at 3 locations; wet insulation confirmed at the south saddle.
- Corrosion under insulation (CUI) confirmed on the shell bottom quadrant: general wall loss with pitting up to 2.1 mm deep.
- UT thickness readings: nominal shell 14.0 mm; minimum recorded 11.2 mm at CML-7 (near south saddle). Corrosion rate estimated 0.35 mm/yr since last survey (2019).
- Retirement thickness per design calc: 9.8 mm. Remaining life at current rate: approx 4 years.

**Recommendations:**
1. Strip and replace insulation at affected areas; apply CUI-resistant coating before reinsulating. Target: next available window.
2. Add CML-7 area to annual UT monitoring (currently 6-yearly).
3. Re-inspect within 12 months. **Next inspection due: 2026-04-03.**

**Inspector:** S. Iyer. Referenced by work order WO-2734 (bundle cleaning).
