# Sulzer OHH — IOM Manual (Extract, Sections 6-8)

## 6. Mechanical Seal Requirements
The cartridge mechanical seal fitted to this pump REQUIRES a continuous, clean external flush per API Plan 32. Flush fluid must be filtered to 25 micron and maintained at a minimum of 1.5 barg above seal chamber pressure.

**Warning:** Operation with interrupted or degraded flush flow will cause seal face overheating and premature mechanical seal failure, typically within 200-400 operating hours. Loss of flush is the single most common cause of seal failure in this pump model.

Recommended checks:
- Verify flush flow indicator FI on the Plan 32 line at every shift.
- Flush line strainer to be cleaned every 3 months, or monthly when pumping waxy or high-viscosity crude.

## 7. Bearings and Lubrication
- Bearing type: SKF 7310 BECBM duplex thrust + NU 310 radial.
- Lubrication: ISO VG 68 oil, oil level to centre of sight glass. Change every 4000 hours.
- Vibration limits: alarm at 7.1 mm/s RMS, trip recommended at 11 mm/s per ISO 10816.

## 8. Minimum Flow
Continuous operation below 130 m3/hr (30% of BEP) causes recirculation cavitation and bearing distress. Ensure minimum-flow line is open during startup per operating procedure.

Spares: seal cartridge P/N SLZ-88412, bearing set P/N SKF-7310-KIT.
