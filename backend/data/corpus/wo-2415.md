# Work Order WO-2415 — P-101A Repeat Seal Failure

**Reported problem:** Second mechanical seal failure on P-101A within 6 months (previous: WO-2301, March 2024). Seal vapor leak detected by field operator; unit rate reduced, near-miss INC-451 raised for the vapor cloud.

**Work performed:**
- Seal cartridge SLZ-88412 replaced (second replacement this year).
- Plan 32 flush line inspected end-to-end: 1/2 inch line found partially plugged with wax approximately 2 m upstream of MOV-118.
- Line cleared by steam lancing, strainer cleaned, flush flow restored to normal.

**Findings:** Failure mode identical to WO-2301 — seal face overheating due to degraded flush flow. Root cause suspected to be waxing of the Plan 32 line, aggravated since crude slate changed to Mangala waxy crude in January 2024. OEM manual (OEM-SLZ-OHH Section 6) warns loss of flush causes seal failure within 200-400 hours.

**Recommendation:** Raise flush line strainer cleaning frequency to monthly; consider heat tracing the Plan 32 line. Referred to A. Verma for RCA. See INC-451 for the associated near-miss investigation.

**Technician:** M. Khan. **Approved by:** A. Verma.
