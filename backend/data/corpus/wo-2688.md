# Work Order WO-2688 — P-101B Scheduled Overhaul (PM)

**Scope:** 4-yearly preventive overhaul of standby crude charge pump P-101B. No process downtime — P-101A carried full load per SOP-012 changeover.

**Work performed:**
- Full strip-down inspection. Impeller wear ring clearances within OEM limits.
- Mechanical seal cartridge replaced on condition (P/N SLZ-88412) — faces serviceable but at mid-life.
- Bearings replaced as preventive (kit SKF-7310-KIT), new ISO VG 68 oil charge.
- Plan 32 flush line for the B pump blown through and confirmed clear; strainer cleaned.
- Coupling alignment set to 0.02 mm.

**Findings:** Pump in good condition. Notably, the B-pump flush line showed early wax film formation similar to the A-pump history (WO-2415) — cleaned as precaution.

**Recommendation:** Include both A and B flush lines in the monthly strainer cleaning route, not only the running pump.

**Technician:** M. Khan. **Approved by:** A. Verma.
