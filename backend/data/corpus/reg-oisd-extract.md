# Regulatory Requirements Index — Unit 300

Summary of statutory and OISD requirements applicable to Unit 300, maintained by the safety department.

## OISD Standards
- **OISD-STD-105 — Work Permit System:** All hot work, confined space and excavation work require permits. Permit records retained 12 months, quarterly audit. Procedures reviewed at minimum every 3 years.
- **OISD-STD-128 — Inspection of Unfired Pressure Vessels:** Registered vessels (V-302, E-104 shell) require periodic external and internal inspection; CUI-susceptible services need targeted external surveys.
- **OISD-STD-132 — Inspection of Pressure Relieving Devices:** PSVs in hydrocarbon service tested at 12-month intervals; extensions only via documented review (MOC).

## Factories Act, 1948
- **Section 31 — Pressure Plant:** Pressure vessels (V-302) to be maintained in safe condition and examined by a competent person at prescribed intervals.
- **Section 36 — Precautions against dangerous fumes:** Confined space entry only with testing, ventilation and supervision (implemented via SOP-034).

## PESO (Petroleum Rules, 2002)
- Petroleum Class A storage (TK-305 area) licensed under PESO; license renewal annually. Fire protection per approved layout.

## Environmental
- Consent to Operate (CTO) from State PCB: flare and VOC monitoring quarterly; hazardous waste (spent seal cartridges, oily sludge from E-104 cleaning) manifested under Hazardous Waste Rules 2016.
