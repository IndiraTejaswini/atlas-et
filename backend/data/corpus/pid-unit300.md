# P&ID Equipment Register — Unit 300 (Crude Distillation)

Reference drawing set: DWG-300-001 Rev 4. Extracted equipment register from P&ID sheets 1-12.

## Rotating Equipment
- P-101A — Crude Charge Pump (main), centrifugal, Sulzer OHH, 450 m3/hr @ 42 barg discharge, 315 kW motor. Mechanical seal with API Plan 32 external flush from the diesel circuit.
- P-101B — Crude Charge Pump (standby), identical to P-101A. Auto-start on low discharge pressure.
- P-105 — Overhead Reflux Pump, 120 m3/hr, 75 kW.

## Static Equipment
- E-104 — Crude Preheat Exchanger, shell & tube, CS shell / SS316 tubes, design 28 barg @ 240 °C. Insulated, hot service.
- C-201 — Atmospheric Distillation Column, 4.2 m diameter, 46 trays, design 3.5 barg @ 380 °C.
- V-302 — Overhead Accumulator, horizontal pressure vessel, design 6.0 barg @ 120 °C. Registered pressure vessel under Factories Act Section 31.
- TK-305 — Intermediate Naphtha Storage Tank, 5000 m3, fixed roof.

## Relief Devices
- PSV-1104 — Pressure Safety Valve on V-302, set pressure 6.0 barg, discharge to flare header. Test interval 12 months per OISD-STD-132.
- PSV-1088 — Pressure Safety Valve on E-104 shell side, set 28 barg.

## Interlocks
- I-301: P-101A/B trip on low suction pressure (below 1.2 barg) to prevent cavitation.
- I-305: V-302 high level alarm at 85%, trip at 95%.

Notes: Plan 32 flush line to P-101A/B seals tapped from diesel rundown; isolation valve MOV-118. Line is 1/2 inch and prone to waxing in winter — see maintenance history.
