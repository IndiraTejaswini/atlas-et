# Inspection Report INSP-102 — Relief Valve Register Review, Unit 300

**Scope:** Annual review of pressure relief device test status per OISD-STD-132 (Inspection of Pressure Relieving Devices). Test interval for PSVs in hydrocarbon service: 12 months unless extended by documented review.

**Findings:**
- PSV-1104 is 5 months past its test due date. The valve protects V-302, a Factories Act Section 31 registered pressure vessel; an overdue relief device is a reportable compliance gap.
- Deferral was informally agreed during the June rate run but never documented through the Management of Change process.

**Recommendations:**
1. Test PSV-1104 at the earliest opportunity; spool spare PSV available in store (S/N RV-2210-04).
2. Any future deferral must go through documented MOC with engineering review per OISD-STD-132 Section 6.
3. V-302 external statutory inspection also falls due 2026-03 — combine outage.
